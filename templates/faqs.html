Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? You might find the answer here. If not, feel free to contact us!

General Platform Information

What is PerkView?

PerkView is a platform where users can watch videos, earn rewards, and engage with content through interactive features.

How does PerkView work?

Users can watch videos, participate in challenges, earn points, and redeem them for rewards.

Sign-Up / Account Related

How do I create an account?

Click on the “Sign Up” button, provide your details, and create an account to get started.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

To change your password, please contact us via the contact page.

Can I change my username or email address?

To change your email or username, please contact us via the contact page.

Earning Rewards

How do I earn rewards?

Earn rewards by watching videos, participating in challenges, and completing other platform activities.

How do I redeem my rewards?

Redeem rewards through the Rewards section on your account page. Choose from prizes or benefits.

Video Uploading & Content Creation

How can I upload a video?

Click on the “Upload Video” button in your account section, choose your video, and follow the prompts to submit it for review.

What types of videos are allowed on PerkView?

Only videos that comply with community guidelines, family-friendly, and original works.

How do I monetize my videos?

You'll get coins each time someone views your video.

Gamification & Challenges(Coming Soon)

What are the challenges and contests?

Challenges are tasks to earn points and rewards. Contests may have specific themes and prizes.

How do I track my progress in challenges?

Track progress in the Challenges section of your profile, which shows completion status and remaining tasks.

Payment & Account

How can I add money to my wallet or make a purchase?

Add funds via your account’s wallet section with preferred payment method.

How can I withdraw my rewards or points?

Go to the Rewards section and choose withdrawal. Redeem rewards for cash, gift cards, or other benefits.

Support & Reporting Issues

I have a technical issue. What should I do?

Contact support via our contact form to report issues or get help with technical problems.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Please report content violating guidelines via our contact form; our team will review it promptly.
